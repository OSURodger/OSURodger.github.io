Entry date 1: 4/11/2021

Apart from the initial bump with learning how to use github, the assignment itself presented little challenge. My solution for figuring out github was to

re-watch the instructor's recorded lessons and search for online guides. When it came to the content of the assignment and my prior knowledge, html formatting

was basically new to me. I have some light experience with code in general that made me feel somewhat familiar with it. The large portion of what I learned was

git-hub itself; I learned how to pull, push, commit, and what a repository is. Of the resources I used in this assignment, I would say the lectures themselves

were the most helpful, followed by web articles. Primarily, I used them to visually see examples of how to structure my work. I did not use either of the

other resources.
